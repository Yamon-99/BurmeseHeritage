## Summary

The Myazedi Inscription (c. 1112 CE) contains four languages: Pali, Mon, Pyu, and Old Burmese.  
The Pyu text is not a separate language. It is an ancient form of Burmese.

## Evidence

- The Pyu word for "son" (သး) matches Old Burmese (သာ).
- The Pyu word for "wife" (မယး) matches Old Burmese (မယာ).
- Dr. Than Tun confirmed in 2004 that Pyu and Bamar are the same people.
